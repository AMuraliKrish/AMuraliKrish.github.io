Prague: The Enchanting City of a Hundred Spires

Nestled along the Vltava River, Prague, the capital of the Czech Republic, exudes an irresistible charm that transports visitors to a fairy-tale setting. Renowned for its picturesque skyline adorned with a hundred spires, Prague is a city where Gothic, Baroque, and Renaissance architecture harmoniously coexist. The historic Old Town Square captivates with its medieval Astronomical Clock and vibrant street performances.
