Gangtok: Tranquil Haven in the Eastern Himalayas

Nestled in the eastern Himalayan range, Gangtok, the capital city of the Indian state of Sikkim, exudes a tranquil charm against the backdrop of snow-capped peaks. Perched at an elevation, this hill station offers a serene escape with its misty mountains, lush landscapes, and a mix of Tibetan and Sikkimese cultures.
