Interlaken: Alpine Paradise between Lakes and Peaks

Situated in the heart of Switzerland, Interlaken is a captivating destination framed by the majestic Swiss Alps and nestled between the serene Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. This charming town serves as a gateway to the Jungfrau region, offering unparalleled views of the iconic Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau peaks.
