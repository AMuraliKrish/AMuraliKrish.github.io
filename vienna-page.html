Vienna: Imperial Elegance and Cultural Opulence

Vienna, Austria's capital, stands as a testament to imperial grandeur and a haven of refined culture. The opulent Schönbrunn Palace, once the summer residence of the Habsburgs, and the splendid Hofburg Palace showcase the city's regal history. St. Stephen's Cathedral, with its iconic spire, dominates the skyline, while the Belvedere Palace hosts a collection of exquisite art.
